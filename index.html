Find all the Waterlilies

"A Kingdom full of Waterlilies suddenly disappears and a Sprite is tasked to find them by travelling across worlds.

Nymphaea is a platform game where the player must guide this Sprite across the world to the portal that leads to another. However, the ticket to enter another universe is to have all the waterlilies of the current world they are in.  Explore and find the Kingdom's waterlilies.”
